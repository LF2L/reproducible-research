Your answer should include a table (or a data frame) with carrier code, median arrival delay, and name of airline. Hint: First calculate the median arrival delay for all carriers, then join with airlines to match airline names. Highest median arrival delay is on Frontier Airlines with 6 minutes, and lowest median arrival delay is on Alaska Airlines with -17 minutes (which means arriving 17 minutes earlier than scheduled).

flights %>%
  filter(!is.na(arr_delay)) %>%
  group_by(carrier) %>%
  summarise(median_arr_delay = median(arr_delay)) %>%
  arrange(desc(median_arr_delay)) %>%
  inner_join(airlines, by = "carrier") %>%
